B.Eng. Student | Robotics & Automation Enthusiast | Embedded Systems Developer

I’m an electrical engineering student specializing in robotics and automation, with a strong interest in combining electronics, programming, and 3D design to create practical, innovative solutions.

My current focus is developing an automatic plant watering system that integrates an ESP32 microcontroller, capacitive soil moisture sensors, servo-driven plant rotation, and a water pump. The system features a custom 3D-printed enclosure and a web-based dashboard for real-time monitoring and control.

Through this and other academic projects, I’ve built hands-on skills in:

  • Hardware: ESP32, Arduino, electronics prototyping, sensor integration
  • Software: C++, JavaScript, Node.js, HTML/CSS
  • Design: SolidWorks, Cura, 3D printing, enclosure design
  • Specialties: IoT development, automation, and mechatronics

Education

  • Bachelor of Electrical Engineering, Robotics and Automation (in progress)
  • Coursework: Control Systems, Embedded Programming, IoT Systems, Electronics
